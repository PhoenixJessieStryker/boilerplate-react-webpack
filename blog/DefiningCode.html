    What is meant by boxifying design?

Boxifying Design is splitting areas of your websites into it's own 'Box' for example

take a piece of paper with a websites homescreen on it and start cutting out set area's

For example

Cutting out the Head of the Design , the centre of the page and finally the bottom

Now identify what may be in those parts for example on a website there is a search bar usually available .. maybe an image

after identitying all these little details you start to see a pattern. You could actually keep cutting up all these features.

You should have found or thought of at least 9-20 possible details that can be split into their own box

This is called Boxifying
